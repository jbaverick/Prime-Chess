Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how Prime Chess ("the App") handles your information. Your privacy is important to us, and we have designed the App to collect as little data as possible.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The App is designed as a "Local-First" application. This means:

  • Game Data: Your game history, win/loss records, chess ratings (ELO), and achievements are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to any servers.
  • Personal Information: If you provide a "Player Name" within the App, it is stored only on your device to personalize your experience.

2. Permissions

To provide its features, the App requires the following permissions:

  • Bluetooth & Location: Used solely for the Local Multiplayer mode. Android requires location access to scan for nearby Bluetooth devices. We do not track or store your physical location.
  • Notifications: Used to provide daily challenges or game reminders if opted-in.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party tracking, analytics (like Google Analytics), or advertising SDKs. When you click on links to our GitHub, Twitter, or Website, you are directed to those respective platforms, which have their own privacy policies.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete all information associated with the App at any time by clearing the App's cache/data in your Android Settings or by uninstalling the App.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at jb.averick@gmail.com.
